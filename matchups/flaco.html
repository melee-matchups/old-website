Gameplan
Try to move around looking for opportunities to whack him when hes going to laser (probably with dash attack / fair), or whiff punish his aerial approaches with grab
Try to powersheild his lazers
When he is dash dancing, you can WD forward down tilt/fsmash

When you get hit by lazer in the air do one of:
1: fair if he is close enough to be hit
2: wave land back / fowards
3: land and react to his option

When you (or falco) get hit by lazer on the ground (or in shield) do one of:
1: dash back and hope to get a grab
2: nair in place
3: wave dash forward down tilt.
