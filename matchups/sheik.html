Sheik


Gameplan
Grab em
dtilt pressure, the dman thing is broken, stay grounded
Can run up and shield, but make sure they dont grab you
Avoid the grab, so don't throw out stupid shit, you'll be grabed
Walling with nair works too

Things to respect
Don't approach with Fair
DI sheik dthrow up and away

Respect (fully charged) needles, try not to get hit for free, and don't run at them when they do them

Things not to respect
She can't punish tipper fsmash on sheild
if the sheik is spamming downsmash on your shield, then you can actually always shieldgrab her as long as you shield DI towards her
If she hits your sheild, you mostly always punish (mabey if she dash backs she can escape, but you can read this or cover it)
if sheik camps, Waveland forward into downtilt

Combos & Punish routes
Keep her in the air with juggles and force her offstage or to the ledge

Edge Guarding
Please grab ledge
If you are facing towards the stage and a d-throw will not allow her to freely grab the ledge, that is your option. If she does not DI properly (which is away), Marth can turnaround dtilt. Her options are extremely limited at this point because if she double-jumps and/or tries to attack, she eats a tipper fsmash (or, occasionally, counter) and loses her stock.

Throw follow ups
Port 1 Throw vs Sheik (Un-staled Throw)
U-throw U-tilt
No DI: 21%-39%
DI in: 22%-30%
DI away:18%-32%
U-throw F/U-air
No DI: 39%-85%
DI in: 27%-76%
DI away: 22%-72%
Dthrow is better than fthrow at low % for a tech chase
At higher %s, can look for F-throw into Pivot F-smash (tech chase if needed)
Fthrow & Bthrow are positinal throws used to set up an edge guard
